Welcome to Rosario, Batangas Tourism Site

Welcome to Rosario, Batangas, where the tranquil embrace of nature meets the vibrant pulse of Filipino hospitality. Nestled on the shores of Batangas Bay, this charming municipality invites you to immerse yourself in its pristine beaches, lush landscapes, and rich cultural heritage.

Explore Rosario, Batangas


Balai Ising

Balai Ising Garden Resort is an ideal staycation or tourist spot destination for all the people who are looking to get away from their city. This resort mainly provides air-conditioned rooms with a kitchen, refrigerator, and grill, allowing visitors to prepare meals while relaxing in the tranquil and sophisticated garden setting.


Batangas Racing Circuit

Batangas Racing Circuit is a permanent circuit in Rosario, Batangas, Philippines that was completed on October 16, 1996. The circuit is 3.7 kilometers long, features 10 turns, and goes clockwise. The circuit is designed to keep the drivers and riders competing in each race, as well as the spectators, safe.


Villa Crisanta Garden Resort

Villa Crisanta Garden Resort is located in Rosario, Batangas. This resort and spa is a one-stop destination for visitors wishing to relax, swim, and enjoy nature. The Pavilion event area can seat up to 400 persons. They also feature a spa and two large pools, one for children and one for adults, which are ideal not just for a summer break but also for amazing poolside parties and team-building events.
